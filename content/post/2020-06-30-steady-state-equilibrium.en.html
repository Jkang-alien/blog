The meaning of \(A^{T}\)

  • Steady state equilibrium
  • Graph Laplacian matrix \(A^{T}CA\)
  • Differential equation and Laplacian matrix
  • Derivative is a graph without branch.
  • Row space and column space are dual.
  • \(A\) and \(A^{T}\) are dual.
