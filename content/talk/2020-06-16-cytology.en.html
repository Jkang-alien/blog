      • Larger nucleus

    • Endocervical cells
      • Cytoplasm diffusely vacuolated or granular

    • Endometrial cell
      • Tight cluster
      • Smaller nucleus
      • Dense heterogeneous apoptotic debris
      • Cytoplasm scant dense vacuolated
      • 3 dimension
